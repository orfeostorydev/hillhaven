Hillview Rise

Located at Hillview Rise just six minutes' walk to Hillview MRT station, this upcoming residential development, Hillhaven, is nestled in a quiet enclave surrounded by Bukit Timah Nature Reserve, Bukit Batok Town Park, Dairy Farm Nature Park and Hindhede Nature Park.A joint venture development by Sekisui House Limited and Far East Organization. Information such as pricing, floor plans and images to be released during the official launch. Click here to register your interest.Follow this page or our social channels to receive official, first-hand updates on Hillhaven.

Visit our Sales Gallery at 100A Upper Bukit Timah Road S589680, 9am to 7pm daily.

Highlights

01

Privacy meets personality

Experience true exclusivity with the Triplex amenitiess. With only 4 units available, these rare 4-bedroom Triplexes start from 2nd Storey and continue up to 4th Storey, with the 3rd Storey opening up to a luxurious swimming pool. Each unit comes with 2 individual car park lots, its own private lift and an exclusive roof terrace.

02

Style and functionality

Every SOHO at Parksuites provide functional spaces with selected units offering platform furniture. All apartments on the 4th Storey feature high ceilings, and these units are designed to be double-storey, dual-key amenitiess, where the upper level can be tenanted without affecting your privacy.

03

Tranquility and serenity

Enter a world that takes care of your family’s wellness. At Parksuites, revel in well-appointed facilities such as the 50-metre Lap Pool, Kid’s Pool, Recreation Pool, Jacuzzi, Gym, BBQ Pavilion and Dining Pavilion. You may also enjoy serene views of the park and beyond. You may look forward to days filled with relaxation and restful moments.
